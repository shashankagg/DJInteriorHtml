Modern living rooms

Elevate your home's central gathering space with our sleek, contemporary living room designs. We blend clean lines, minimalist aesthetics, and functional layouts to create sophisticated modern environments that balance style with comfort. Our approach incorporates carefully selected materials—from polished concrete and brushed metals to warm woods and textural fabrics—all working together to achieve visual harmony while maintaining a welcoming atmosphere.

Each contemporary living room is thoughtfully curated to reflect your personal lifestyle needs and design sensibilities, resulting in a refined space that feels both cutting-edge and timeless. Our attention to lighting, spatial flow, and architectural details ensures your modern living area becomes the striking yet comfortable centerpiece of your home.


Home Theaters

Transform your recreational space into an immersive entertainment experience with our custom movie room designs. We combine premium audio-visual technology with thoughtful interior elements to create the perfect cinematic environment. Our designs feature strategic lighting, acoustic treatments, and comfortable seating arrangements that maximize both the technical performance and aesthetic appeal of your home theater.

Each movie room is uniquely crafted to accommodate your specific space constraints, technical requirements, and design preferences, resulting in a personalized entertainment hub that will impress family and friends while providing years of enjoyment.


Accent Walls

A striking accent wall can transform any room from ordinary to extraordinary. Our accent wall designs add depth, character, and visual interest to your space with carefully selected colors, textures, and patterns. Whether you prefer bold geometric designs, subtle textured finishes, or dramatic color statements, our team delivers custom solutions that perfectly complement your existing décor while making a powerful statement.

Each project is custom-designed to meet your specific needs and aesthetic preferences, ensuring a result that not only looks stunning but also reflects your personal style.

Pricing Model

Services Description
Introduction Call No Charge We begin our process with understanding the specific challenges that our customers are trying to address to make sure we can provide the best service. Our first 30 minute introduction call is obligation free, with the option to set up a video or audio call. We support Zoom, FaceTime and WhatsApp Video.
Consultation $100/hour For the next steps, we can either build a design based on in-person visit (1 hour charge @$100/hour) or photo and video input wherever feasible. This visitation charge will be discounted for the clients who are moving forward with design services.
Full-room Design $350-$600 A typical single room design is estimated at 4-5 hours of design and research work, and clients can expect a 5D software rendering, up to 2 revisions and links to all items discussed as part of design. Physical delivery of items or installation is not included.

We do offer multi-room discounts for our clients.
Custom Builds Pricing varies We will work with the clients to give an estimate if they'd like to include delivery, accessory and art purchases and customization. For custom builds like accent walls, built-in cabinetry etc. we will provide estimates and contract terms.
