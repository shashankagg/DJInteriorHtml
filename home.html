Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us

How much does interior design cost in Seattle?

At Luminart Design Studio, we keep pricing transparent and accessible. Our free 30-minute introduction call helps us understand your project before any commitment. From there:

  • Consultation visit: $99/hour (credited when you proceed with design services)
  • Single room design (consultation only): $299 to $499
  • Single room design with furniture, accessories, and layout: $499 to $4,999+, depending on selections
  • Accent wall or custom wall design: $1,999 to $4,999+, depending on size and complexity
  • Full room package (design + wall build + furnishings): $4,999 to $9,999+

We offer multi-room discounts and always provide a detailed estimate before starting. Every design includes 5D software renderings, 2 revisions, and sourced product links. We accept cash, check, and Venmo.

What is light art in interior design?

Light art is the integration of light sources into architectural elements and design features like accent walls, display shelving, and entertainment spaces. Our projects frequently combine 3D textures, natural materials, and concealed lighting to produce a focal point that doubles as a living art piece. When done well, ambient light elevates a room's mood and character, turning an ordinary space into something refined and inviting. It's our signature specialty and one of the most requested features from our clients.

What does the interior design process look like?

Our process is built around collaboration and transparency:

  1. Free introduction call (30 min): We listen to your needs, preferences, and budget over Zoom, FaceTime, or WhatsApp.
  2. Consultation: We visit your space in person ($99/hour) or work from photos and video. This fee is credited when you proceed with design services.
  3. Concept development: We create mood boards, color schemes, and 5D software renderings so you can visualize the design before committing.
  4. Material and furniture selection: We collaborate on finishes, colors, furniture, decor, and accessories that match your style.
  5. Revisions: We provide 2 revisions to the design at no extra cost to ensure we fine-tune everything to your vision.
  6. Procurement and installation: For custom builds like accent walls and built-in cabinetry, we handle sourcing, contractor coordination, and installation.
  7. Final reveal and touch-ups: We walk you through the completed space and address any final adjustments.

Open communication is key throughout. Our goal is to make the process easy and enjoyable, not overwhelming.

Do I need an interior designer for just one room?

Absolutely. Single-room makeovers are our most popular service. Whether it's a living room refresh, a bedroom redesign, a home theater build, or a statement accent wall, we deliver full designs starting at $299. Many clients begin with one room and come back for more after seeing the transformation. We also offer multi-room discounts when you're ready to expand the scope. No project is too small for thoughtful, personalized design.
